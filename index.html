FAQ

You must receive an enrollment letter from Hogwarts, which explains how to get to Platform 9¾ and board the Hogwarts Express.

Students receive essential magical items including wands, robes, textbooks, and house-specific items like the Gryffindor sword or Slytherin robe.

Hogwarts is private and magical. Visitors are generally not allowed unless accompanied by a student or during special events.

Hogwarts teaches Potions, Defense Against the Dark Arts, Transfiguration, Charms, Herbology, and Care of Magical Creatures, among other magical subjects.

Students are sorted into Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, or Slytherin by the Sorting Hat based on personality, values, and traits.

Hogwarts enforces rules strictly. Consequences may include detention, loss of house points, or other disciplinary measures depending on the severity of the violation.
